Proximity

Company Name

Xfinity

How Used

Xfinity excercises the principle of proximity greatly as it dsiplays movies for users to choose from. As shown in the mobile browser, there are small horizontal gaps between movies in a certain category, and slightly larger, vertical gaps between movie categories. This makes the selection process easier on the eyes of the user.
