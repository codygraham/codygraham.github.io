Contrast

Company Name

NBA - Portland Trail Blazers

How Used

The NBA website showcases contrast as seen on this screenshot of the Trail Blazers home page. The NBA does a great job of using its main color scheme (blue and white) to become gradient with the Blazers team colors and logo. All information is displayed on white background and all navigation, headers and footers show up on blue strips that reach across most of the page.
